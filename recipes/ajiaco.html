Ajiaco

Description

This recipe has always been one of my favorite recipes. Whenever my mother would offer to make some I simply could not say no. The rich creamy taste is to die for!

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breast (skin removed)
  • 12 cups of water
  • 3 ears fresh corn (cut into 2 pieces)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 chicken bouillon cubes
  • 3 scallion
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 3 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 2 cups papa criolla (Andean Potato)
  • 3 medium white potatoes (peeled and sliced)
  • 3 medium red potatoes (peeled and sliced)
  • 1/3 cup guascas
  • 1 cup heavy cream for serving
  • 1 cup capers for serving
